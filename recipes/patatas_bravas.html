Patatas Bravas

Description

Patatas Bravas is a popular Spanish tapas recipe of cripsy oven-baked potatoes served with a tangy, spicy tomato sauce (salsa brava) and aioli or guacamole. Perfect as a simple appetizer, quick snack or side dish.

Ingredients (measurements not specified)

Roasted Potatoes

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Spicy Tomato Sauce (Aioli)

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Chili
  • Paprika Powder
  • Tomatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Agave Syrup
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into bite-sized pieces and pre-cook them in a pot of salted water and baking powder for 5-7 minutes. Then drain the cooking water, making sure the potatoes are very dry.
  2. Add oil, salt, and pepper, then shake the lidded pot untill everything is well combined.
  3. Spread the seasoned potatoes on a preheated baking tray and bake at 428 F (220 C) for 25-30 minutes.
  4. For the tomato sauce, first saute the onions for 2-3 minutes. Then add garlic and chilli, and saute for another minute.
  5. Then add paprika powder and saute briefly before adding tomatoes, vinegar, agave syrup, salt and some pepper.
  6. Cover the pan and simmer for 5 minutes, then optionally puree untill smooth using a blender.
  7. Roast the Pimienntos de Padron in a pan with a little oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve.
